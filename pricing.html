FAQs

Pricing questions, answered

Can I switch between monthly and annual billing?

Absolutely. You can change billing cadence from the admin portal at any time. Annual contracts are prorated and we credit any unused time.

Do you offer nonprofit or startup discounts?

Yes. Startups under $2M ARR receive 40% off Pro for the first year. Nonprofits receive custom pricing. Contact sales to verify eligibility.

What’s included in onboarding services?

Onboarding includes data migration, automation setup, training sessions, and QA. Enterprise plans receive a dedicated solution architect.

How secure is my data with PulseCRM?

We’re compliant with SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, and GDPR. Enterprise plans unlock data residency, custom retention, and bring-your-own-key.
