Our story

From spreadsheets to a unified CRM

PulseCRM began as a humble internal tool built by a revenue operations team to consolidate disparate systems into a single, intelligent workspace. When their peers asked for access, it was clear a modern CRM needed to adapt to the way teams actually collaborate.

2019 · Spark

Built prototype to eliminate duplicate records and manual reporting across a global SaaS company.

2020 · Expansion

Seed funding allowed us to launch automation, collaborative playbooks, and data enrichment modules.

2022 · Scale

Introduced AI-powered forecasting and expanded to EMEA and APAC with localized support.

2024 · Platform

Launched Pulse Marketplace for 120+ integrations and unveiled our interactive dashboard demo.
