Privacy Policy

Protecting your data is core to our mission

We build PulseCRM with trust, transparency, and compliance at the heart of every decision. Our privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Home / Privacy Policy
Last updated January 1, 2026

We review our policy annually and whenever regulations change.

1. Introduction

PulseCRM (“we”, “us”, “our”) provides customer relationship management software and services. This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard personal data when you interact with our website, products, or services.

2. Data we collect

We collect information you provide directly, data we receive automatically, and information obtained from third-party sources.

  • Account data: Name, email, company, role, billing information.
  • Product usage: Log events, device metadata, feature interactions, and support cases.
  • Marketing data: Preferences, form submissions, event participation, and communication opt-ins.
  • Third-party data: Integrations, enrichment partners, and security screening providers.

3. How we use data

We process personal data to deliver and improve our services while honoring your privacy choices.

  • Provide, maintain, and optimize the PulseCRM platform.
  • Authenticate users, prevent fraud, and ensure platform security.
  • Deliver customer support, training, and onboarding.
  • Send updates, marketing communications, and product announcements (opt-out anytime).
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce agreements.

4. Legal bases for processing

We rely on various lawful bases under GDPR and other regulations:

  • Contract: Processing necessary to provide contracted services.
  • Legitimate interests: Improving product experience, securing systems, and communicating relevant updates.
  • Consent: Collecting marketing preferences and optional analytics.
  • Legal obligations: Meeting regulatory and compliance requirements.

5. Data retention

We retain personal data as long as necessary to deliver services, fulfill legal obligations, or resolve disputes. Customers control retention policies within the platform. Upon termination, we delete or anonymize data within 60 days unless otherwise agreed.

6. Sharing and transfers

We share data with vetted subprocessors and partners that support our operations. We prohibit selling customer data. International transfers leverage standard contractual clauses or other legally recognized safeguards.

  • Infrastructure providers (AWS, GCP) for hosting and storage.
  • Customer support tools and analytics services.
  • Integration partners authorized by customers.
  • Legal or regulatory authorities when required by law.

7. Security

PulseCRM implements technical and organizational measures including encryption, role-based access controls, penetration testing, and secure development standards. Customers can request our security overview or SOC 2 report.

8. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have data rights such as access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, and objection. Submit requests to privacy@pulsecrm.com. We respond within 30 days.

9. Cookies and tracking

We use cookies to remember preferences, analyze usage, and personalize content. Manage cookies via browser settings or our in-app preferences center.

10. Children's privacy

PulseCRM is not directed to children under 16. If you believe we have collected information about a minor, contact us to delete the data.

11. Contact us

For privacy inquiries, email privacy@pulsecrm.com or write to PulseCRM Privacy Team, 600 Market Street, San Francisco, CA 94105, USA.
