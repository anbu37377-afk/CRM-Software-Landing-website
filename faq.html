Getting started

How long does implementation take?

Most teams launch within 30 days. Our onboarding includes data import, workspace configuration, automation setup, and live training. Enterprise engagements can include a phased rollout with change management.

Do you offer a sandbox or trial?

Yes, every account receives a 14-day fully featured trial. Admins can also enable sandbox environments for testing new automation and integrations before deploying to production.

Can PulseCRM migrate data from Salesforce, HubSpot, or Zendesk?

We have migration templates for major CRMs and support custom imports via CSV or APIs. Our success team can assist with mapping fields, deduplicating records, and validating imported data.

Pricing & billing

What payment methods do you support?

We accept major credit cards, ACH transfers, and invoice billing for annual contracts. Enterprise plans can provision purchase orders.

Can we adjust seats mid-term?

You can add or remove seats anytime. Additions are prorated, and reductions take effect at the next renewal unless otherwise negotiated.

Do you provide discounts for nonprofits or startups?

We offer tailored pricing for qualified nonprofits and startups under $2M ARR. Contact sales to verify eligibility and discuss options.

Security & compliance

Where is PulseCRM hosted?

PulseCRM runs on SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure with regional data centers in the US, EU, and APAC. Enterprise plans support data residency controls.

What compliance frameworks do you meet?

We maintain SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, GDPR readiness, and HIPAA alignment. Enterprise customers can bring their own encryption keys and configure custom retention policies.

How do you protect customer data?

We use encryption at rest and in transit, role-based access controls, audit logs, anomaly detection, and routine penetration testing. Optional add-ons include SSO, SCIM, and advanced permissioning.

Integrations & automation

Does PulseCRM integrate with Slack, Google Workspace, and Microsoft 365?

Yes. We offer native integrations for Slack, Gmail, Google Calendar, Outlook, Teams, and more. Administrators can manage permissions and channels centrally.

Can we build custom automations?

Use Flow Builder to connect triggers, conditions, and actions across sales, marketing, and support. Developers can extend workflows via APIs, webhooks, and Lambda functions.

How often does PulseCRM sync with other systems?

Most integrations sync in near real time. You can configure custom sync intervals and use change data capture for mission-critical systems.
